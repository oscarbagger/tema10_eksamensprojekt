About

Line Engelhardt

An illustrator based in Copenhagen.
I take pride in my analog work, using traditional tools like graphite pencils and charcoal. My drawing style has been described as very sketchy and explosive.
You can often find me working on the floor, surrounded by all my favourite art tools.

Exhibitions

Vera, Frederiksberg.

Centro Fotográfico Manuel Álvarez Bravo, Oaxaxa, Mexico.
